This table displays the volumes of clients' deposits and withdrawals by their registration countries. The data is displayed by periods of one month. Not more than (customizable) countries with the highest total flow of funds are displayed. If a request contains more than 12 months, only data for the last 12 months is displayed in the table. All values are shown in (customizable) currency for ease of comparison. Depositing to an account by clients is displayed in green. Fund withdrawals are displayed in red.

Deposits and Withdrawals graphs display the total volume of funds deposited and withdrawn by clients as percentage shares. Not more than (customizable) countries with the highest total value of funds deposited and withdrawn by clients for the entire period are displayed. All values are shown in (customizable) for ease of comparison.
The graph allows assessing the overall trend in depositing trading accounts by clients. The period applied in the graph depends on the interval. If a request contains up to 30 days, each graph point displays one-day values. In case there are up to 60 days in a request, results for one week are shown and, finally, if there are more than 60 days, each point stands for one-month values. The graph consists of three lines:
  • Amount line shows the amount of funds deposited by clients at each interval. All values are shown in (customizable) for more convenience;
  • Count line shows the amount of funds depositing operations at each interval;
  • Average line shows the average value of funds deposited to accounts at each interval.

The table provides an opportunity to evaluate the company's profit based on the clients turnover. The data is displayed in blocks for each client group showing amount for each month.
  • Group column contains the name of a client group;
  • Date column displays the month, for which all the total amounts for the group are shown;
  • Deposits column shows the amount of all funds deposited by the specified group's clients to their accounts for the appropriate month in this group's currency;
  • Withdrawals column shows the amount of all funds withdrawn by the specified group's clients from their accounts for the appropriate month in this group's currency;
  • Deposits-Withdrawals column displays the difference between the amount of deposited and withdrawn funds in this group's currency;
  • Deals count column displays the number of deals performed by clients;
  • Turnover column shows trade turnover of performed deals in the account group's currency. Calculation: number of lots * contract size. Then, the obtained value is reduced to the single currency;
  • Revenue column shows the company's revenue from every million of the client's trade turnover. Calculation: (Deposits - Withdrawals) * 1 000 000 / Turnover. Currency is specified in the next column;
  • Currency column displays the account group's currency.
